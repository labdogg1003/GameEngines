Painted Dungeon Set

Quickly create environments using this pack of over 150 modular prefabs with hand-painted textures. To use, simply drag and drop included prefabs into your scene. Use Unity's vertex snap (hold down the v key) for easy positioning.

Features:
-20 painted textures plus alternates and recolors.
-Stone textures included in two color themes.

-Main textures range from 512x512 to 512x1024 in size, while smaller objects use 256x265 to 256x512.
-Over 150 modular prefabs including floors, walls, and detail objects.
-Objects can be quickly snapped together to make laying out your level easy.
